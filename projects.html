Process

How the work is approached

Step 01

Frame the problem clearly

Start with the target outcome, define what success looks like, and identify the data signals that actually matter before choosing a model.

Step 02

Prepare and understand the data

Cleaning, transforming, and exploring the dataset usually creates the biggest lift in model quality, so this stage gets real attention.

Step 03

Train, evaluate, and explain

Compare approaches, measure results with the right metrics, and keep the final output readable enough for both technical and non-technical audiences.
